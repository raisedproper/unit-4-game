Danny's Crystal Collector

Guess what numbers are behind the crystals. Here's how the game works:

There will be four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. You will be shown a random number at the start of the game.

Each time you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the your total score.

The game will hide this amount until the player clicks a crystal. When you do click one, your score counter will update.

You win if your total score matches the random number from the Random Number Result. You lose if your score goes above the random number. The game will restarts when the when you win or lose.
